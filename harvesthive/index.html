What we do?

Our website provides a platform for farmers to sell their crops directly to customers, eliminating the need for intermediaries. It also offers location-based recommendations on which crops are suitable to grow, and educates farmers on new advancements in the field. Overall, our website aims to empower farmers by giving them access to market information and knowledge to improve their farming practices.

How we do?

Our website uses a combination of data analysis and agricultural expertise to provide location-based recommendations on suitable crops to grow. It also features a marketplace where farmers can list their crops for sale directly to customers. Additionally, the website offers educational resources and updates on advancements in the agricultural industry, keeping farmers informed and up-to-date on best practices.

Why we do?

Our website is motivated by a desire to help farmers. By providing a platform for direct sales, farmers can earn more and reduce their dependence on intermediaries. Location-based recommendations help farmers grow crops that are more likely to thrive, leading to higher yields and increased profitability. Educational resources keep farmers informed of new advancements in the field, leading to improved farming practices. Overall, our website aims to empower farmers by providing them with access to market information and knowledge.

What you must do?

Farmers can use the website to sell their crops directly to customers, bypassing intermediaries and earning more. They can also use the location-based recommendations to decide which crops are suitable to grow in their area, leading to higher yields and increased profitability. The educational resources available on the website can help farmers improve their farming practices and stay informed of new advancements in the field. Overall, the website provides farmers with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their farming operations.
